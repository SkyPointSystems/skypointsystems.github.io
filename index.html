Our Services

Security Risk Snapshot

An on-site and Microsoft 365 assessment that documents exactly what you have and where you're exposed. You'll receive a written reference packet — asset inventory, network map, and a prioritized list of real risks — plus a walkthrough call to explain it all in plain language.

What's Included

  • On-site inventory of all workstations, laptops, servers, printers, and network equipment — including make, model, serial number, and end-of-life status
  • Microsoft 365 environment audit — user accounts, admin roles, MFA status, licensing, and Secure Score
  • A written reference packet you keep — asset inventory, a simple network diagram, and your top 5–8 prioritized risks explained in plain language, not technical jargon
  • A walkthrough call to go over the findings together and answer questions

What This Isn't

A sales pitch disguised as an assessment. We document and explain — we don't push you into a bigger engagement unless there's a genuine need for one.

Law Firm Security Baseline & Hardening

A structured, fixed-scope engagement to strengthen Microsoft 365 security controls. Includes MFA enforcement, admin role review, conditional access hardening, email security review, backup verification, and a clear, prioritized remediation roadmap.

Fractional Security Oversight

Ongoing security oversight for law firms that want continued protection without hiring a full-time engineer. Includes monthly posture review, Secure Score monitoring, patch and backup verification, and quarterly executive review meetings.

Focused Advisory Only

We do not provide general IT support, helpdesk services, or emergency incident response. Our focus is on security hardening and structured oversight — working alongside your existing IT provider.
